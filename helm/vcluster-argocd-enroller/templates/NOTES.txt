  kubectl logs -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "vcluster-argocd-enroller.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }} -n {{ include "vcluster-argocd-enroller.vclusterNamespace" . }}

The operator will:
1. Watch for vCluster StatefulSets in the configured namespace
2. Extract kubeconfig from vCluster secrets
3. Create corresponding cluster secrets in ArgoCD
4. Clean up ArgoCD secrets when vClusters are deleted
